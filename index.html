About us
Ikotun Senior High School:Origins

Ikotun senior high school was founded in 1998. It has participated in different competitions and is one of the best in district 1. It has a lot of awards from competitons like spelling bee and rotary club. The pioneer teachers are now principals and vice principals of their respective schools.

The school has had a lot of students in recent times but nonetheless strived to maintain its name.coming to the school has been one of my joys in recent times
